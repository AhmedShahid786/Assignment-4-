SWF is a Pakistani NGO initiated to serve the nation in different departments of life. Currently SWF is opertaing in the capital city of Karachi but we hope to spread our services all over the nation to serve our homeland in true meanings.     • Opportunity For Every Person
    • Trusted National And Multinational Firms
